What kind of materials does the packaging usually consist of?

According to relevant research, 40% of express parcels are plastic bags, 50% are cardboard boxes, 10% are document seals. According to this estimate, the express plastic bags consume a total of 480,000 tons of polyethylene, equivalent to the consumption of 20 million tons of oil.
