Some indirect consequences of overpackaging:

1. Many plastic materials cannot be effectively recycled and degraded, resulting in the destruction of land and marine ecosystems

2. The large carbon emissions from packaging pose a challenge to climate change

3. Waste of human resources from handling a large amount of waste sorting

4. Packaging waste is put into the ocean, causing microplastic pollution and posing a threat to human health

5. The deforestation of trees poses a threat to the terrestrial ecosystem
